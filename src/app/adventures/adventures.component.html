Cliff Jump into Jacob's Well

If you are a fan of the fantasy under the water, Jacob's Well is the place to be. The rich blue water and the sunlight hitting the depths of the well's floor is a beautiful sight to see.
Depth of the well
Jacob's Well is located in Wimberly, Texas. It is characterized to be a dangerous diving spot, so knowing the map of the area is important before you go!

Space Center Houston

Our personal favorite, the Space Center in Houston, Texas is a great place to visit with family and friends. If you are looking to expand your knowledge about space, or just in the mood for some fun,this is the perfect place to visit. NASA strives to give its visitors a spoonful of what goes on on site for them!

The Hobbit's Cafe in Houston Texas

The Hobbit Cafe in Houston is exactly what it sounds like. They serve the best food, but in "Hobbit" size. Their menu consists of large burgers, frieds, milkshakes, and other classic western foods.

Six Flags over Texas

Six Flags over Texas is an adventure one can never miss. Rollercoasters like the New Texas Giant, Titan, and the Shock Wave are staples that one must go on. Cotton Candy, funnel cake, and drinks are some treats you can binge on while you're there, but make sure you eat after you go on the rollercoasters :)

Texas Bluebonnets

The texas Bluebonnets are a must see when you visit Texas during the Spring time. Go with family and friends and take pictures to collect memories.
