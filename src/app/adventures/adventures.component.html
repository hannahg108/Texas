Cliff Jump into Jacob's Well

If you are a fan of the fantasy under the water, Jacob's Well is the place to be. The rich blue water and the sunlight hitting the depths of the well's floor is a beautiful sight to see.
Depth of the well
Jacob's Well is located in Wimberly, Texas. It is characterized to be a dangerous diving spot, so knowing the map of the area is important before you go!

Space Center Houston

Our personal favorite, the Space Center in Houston, Texas is a great place to visit with family and friends. If you are looking to expand your knowledge about space, or just in the mood for some fun,this is the perfect place to visit. NASA strives to give its visitors a spoonful of what goes on on site for them!
