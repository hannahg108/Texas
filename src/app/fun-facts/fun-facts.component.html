School Traditions

  • Homecoming Mums

    Homecoming mums appeared in Texas around the 1930s, and they began as a sweet token given to a girl by her date in honor of the high school homecomng game and events. As the tradition grew in popularity so did the elaborate design of the mums. Eventually girls began to take matter into their own hands and would either make their own mums or bought them for themselves.

  • Fair Day

    The biggest fair in America is the State Fair of Texas. Which is why fair day is such a big deal. Each year on a friday when the state fair is occuring all elementary student receive a fair ticket and get the day off to enjoy the amazing fair. The following week on friday the same occurs for Middle schools and high schools.
















Interesting creations

  • Dr Pepper

    It was created by pharmacist Charles Alderton in Waco Texas in the 1880s. It is said that charles liked the smell of all the different fruit syrups and wanted to make a drink that tasted like that smell. That is how he came up with the first Dr Pepper.

  • Time Capsule

    On the 100th anniversary of the discovery of helium the Helium Centennial Time Columns Monument was built in 1968. Each leg of the sculpture is a time capsule, with artifacts, letters, and knowledge from 1968 stored inside in an inert helium atmosphere. One of the time capsule is said to contains a passbook to a bank account expected to be worth $1 quadrillion by the time it opens in 2968.
